About Our Charity

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to improving lives in African communities through sustainable development projects.

Our Mission and Vision

Founded in 2010, African Charity has been working tirelessly to address the most pressing challenges facing African communities. Our mission is to create sustainable solutions that empower individuals and transform communities.

We believe in a future where every African child has access to quality education, clean water, and proper healthcare. Our vision is to break the cycle of poverty through community-led development initiatives.

With projects in 12 African countries, we've impacted over 500,000 lives through our various programs. Our approach focuses on sustainability, community involvement, and measurable impact.
